Welcome to the DJ Black's website

Student in engineering school, DJ Black is now a generalist DJ for over 7 years. His real name is Guillaume LENOIR. He hosts different evenings for individuals or bars, clubs (Atlantic Concorde, Bar Rock ...). He often carries out integration weekends, student parties galas or even birthdays, weddings of evenings of apartments.

DJ Black is very versatile and can adapt to your request if you have a specific style thanks to the richness of its database. It will provide you with a sample of its playlist to allow you to verify that it matches your tastes. Satisfying its customers is also one of its priorities, you can look at its opinions in the "opinion" section. If you wish, you can contact him to check his availability and discuss the nature of your project. He will be able to answer all your questions.
